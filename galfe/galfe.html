Galaxy Evolution

Occupation
Dark matter forms halos. Other matter follows the dark matter and collapses inside their halos, forming galaxies. Properties of halos and galaxies are linked, assembly bias. Plays a role in how galaxies are linked
Star-Formation
Forming stars is a important part of galaxies life. Requires gas, exhausts gas. Leaves red stars, "passive" end of life. Study the distribution of hydrogen, an ingredient in star formation.
