About Me

I'm a third-year graduate student in the Astronomy Department at University of Maryland - College Park. I currently work with Prof. Benedikt Diemer researching a better framework to study dark matter in universe-scale simulations.

Before then, I attended the University of Wisconsin-Madison majoring in astronomy, physics, and mathematics with a minor in computer science. While there, I researched radio telescope instrumentation with Prof. Peter Timbie and large-scale structure in hydrodynamical simulations with Prof. Elena D'Onghia.

For more details on my research adventures or ways to find me, please see the sections below! If you want to get to know me a little better, I include some personal interests and hobbies in the About Me page.
