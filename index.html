Research

Mapping the Universe

By observing the intensity of the radio signal from space, we can measure the distribution of galaxies throughout the universe. We plan to build a universe map from these measurements, providing key insight about how these galaxies form and how dark energy influences their distribution.

  • Learn More

Unraveling the Dynamics of Dark Matter

I develop software that tracks the movement of dark matter in computer simulations, examining how the dynamics of a galaxy's underlying dark matter can imprint on the shape of a galaxy. Recognizing these features in observations supplies valuable clues on the nature of dark matter.
